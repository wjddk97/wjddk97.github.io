```python
nums = [5, 1, 3, 7, 2, 9]

for i in range(1, len(nums)):
    j = i - 1
    cNum = nums[i]

    while nums[j] > cNum and j >= 0:
        nums[j+1] = nums[j]
        j -= 1
      
    nums[j+1] = cNum


-----
```
